Listening to a 60-minute podcast typically takes an hour, meaning key insights are often missed or postponed. That’s a huge time cost for professionals who need quick, actionable takeaways.

PODLITE is a pipeline that first transcribes audio with Whisper, then uses LLMs to automatically summarize the episode, extract key points, and tag speakers. It produces a structured “show notes” output with high-level takeaways and next-step actions.

Users report getting the same strategic value in 5 minutes as they would from a full episode (≈12× speedup), and they can quickly locate the specific segment they need without listening through noise. This saves time, improves retention, and makes podcast content actionable.
