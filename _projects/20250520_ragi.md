Searching a long manual or contract can take 30+ minutes, and generic chatbots often provide confident but incorrect answers (“hallucinations”) that lead to wasted follow-up work.

RAGI is built as a Retrieval-Augmented Generation assistant: it first searches the source documents for the most relevant passages, then generates answers that cite the exact text used. That means every response is traceable back to the original document.

In practice, RAGI cuts research time from hours to minutes, reduces “confidence without correctness” risk, and gives users a reliable workflow for making decisions based on verifiable information.
